Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on a rafting trip?

We recommend water shoes, quick-dry clothing, sunscreen, a hat, and a reusable water bottle. Avoid cotton and bring a change of clothes for after the trip.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No prior experience is needed! Our guides provide safety instructions and training before each trip, making it beginner-friendly.

Are meals included in the package?

Yes, most of our full-day and overnight trips include meals. Check the trip details or contact us to confirm.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Rafting trips still run in light rain. In case of severe weather or unsafe conditions, we will reschedule or offer a refund.
